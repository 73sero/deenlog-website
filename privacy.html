4 · iOS App — Data Storage

Your tracking data — salah logs, adhkar & dhikr counts, Quran khatm progress, fasting marks, supplement intakes, habits, todos, schedules, notes, progress, and backups — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate user accounts or analytics servers. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers.

5 · iOS App — Notifications

If you enable notifications — salah times, Tahajjud, supplement reminders, todo alerts — they are scheduled locally on your device by iOS. No notification data is sent to any server.

6 · iOS App — Calendar Export

If you use the calendar export feature, events are written directly to your device’s calendar via Apple’s EventKit framework. No calendar data is sent to any server we operate.

7 · iOS App — Prayer Times, Qibla & Location

If you enable prayer times, DeenLog may ask for location permission. Depending on your settings, approximate coordinates may be sent to one of two third-party services to retrieve prayer times:

  • Mawaqit — if you select a specific mosque. Operated by Mawaqit, France.
  • Aladhan — if you use a calculation method (e.g. Muslim World League, ISNA). Operated by Islamic Network.

These requests are optional, made on demand, and never used for analytics or advertising. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of the feature you requested) and Art. 6(1)(a) (consent for location access). You can use the app without enabling prayer times at all.

Qibla compass. If you open the Qibla feature, DeenLog uses your location to calculate the direction and distance to the Kaaba, the device compass (magnetometer) to point the way, and an optional sun-position check that is computed locally on your device. The Qibla screen shows a small map rendered through Apple’s MapKit framework; to load map tiles, Apple Inc. (One Apple Park Way, Cupertino, CA, USA) may receive the map region. We operate no server in this process and store no Qibla or location data. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent for location access) and Art. 6(1)(b). Apple’s handling of MapKit data is described in Apple’s privacy policy.

Verse of the Day, Calendar, Milestones. These features run entirely on your device. No personal data is sent to any server we operate.

8 · iOS App — Widgets & App Group

DeenLog uses an iOS App Group to share data with its widgets (Dhikr counter, next prayer, today’s habits, Hijri date, next supplement). All shared data stays on your device. Widgets do not make any network requests on their own.

9 · No Tracking, No Analytics

Neither the website nor the iOS app uses any of the following:

  • Third-party advertising SDKs
  • Behavioural or product analytics (Firebase, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Marketing trackers, pixels, or fingerprinting
  • A/B testing platforms

The only outbound network calls from the iOS app are the optional prayer-time lookups described in § 7.

12 · Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — obtain a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — have inaccurate data corrected
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — “right to be forgotten”
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) — at any time, without affecting prior lawfulness

To exercise any of these rights, contact us by email (see § 1). We respond within one month, as required by Art. 12(3) GDPR.

Because most processing happens on your device, the technical exercise of many rights is direct: deleting the app erases all app data; clearing your browser data erases the language preference. We are happy to confirm in writing.

Qibla-Kompass. Wenn du die Qibla-Funktion öffnest, nutzt DeenLog deinen Standort, um Richtung und Entfernung zur Kaaba zu berechnen, den Geräte-Kompass (Magnetometer) zur Ausrichtung sowie eine optionale Sonnenstands-Prüfung, die lokal auf deinem Gerät berechnet wird. Der Qibla-Screen zeigt eine kleine Karte über Apples MapKit-Framework; zum Laden der Kartenkacheln kann Apple Inc. (One Apple Park Way, Cupertino, CA, USA) die Kartenregion erhalten. Wir betreiben dabei keinen Server und speichern keine Qibla- oder Standortdaten. Rechtsgrundlage: Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. a DSGVO (Einwilligung für Standortzugriff) sowie lit. b. Apples Umgang mit MapKit-Daten ist in Apples Datenschutzerklärung beschrieben.

Ayah des Tages, Kalender, Meilensteine. Diese Funktionen laufen vollständig auf deinem Gerät. Es werden keine personenbezogenen Daten an einen von uns betriebenen Server gesendet.
